Core Loop
Do carefully observe traffic and cross streets - Get points and score multipliers - Unlock/Prepare harder streets with faster, crazier vehicles - Repeat

New Mechanic Progression (Risk Multiplier)

Teach: Player crosses a street with slightly faster cars and sees a small x2 multiplier

Test: Streets introduce mixed speeds and tighter gaps to maintain the multiplier

Twist: No traffic lights; player must rely on sound and visual cues to keep the multiplier

Master: Extremely fast and unpredictable traffic where only perfect timing keeps the bonus alive
